Formula Glossary

Required Hours

Converts forecast volume and AHT into workload hours.

Required Hours = (Forecast Volume × AHT) / 3600

Volume-Based Required HC

Uses required hours, productive hours, occupancy, and buffer to estimate required HC.

Required HC = (Required Hours / (Productive Hours per HC × Occupancy)) × (1 + Buffer %)

Planning Mode Logic

Switches between demand-based or manual headcount requirement.

Volume Based = Volume HC | HC Based = Manual Required HC | Hybrid = Volume HC + Manual Required HC

Total Shrinkage %

Combines planned and unplanned shrinkage assumptions.

Total Shrinkage % = Planned Shrinkage % + Unplanned Shrinkage %

Graduate HC

HC graduating from training after training duration and pass rate adjustment.

Graduate HC = Actual Class HC × Pass Rate %

Nesting Effective HC

Partial productivity contribution from HC currently in nesting.

Nesting Effective HC = Nesting HC × Nesting Productivity %

SME Effective HC

Counts only the productive portion of SME HC.

Percent Mode: SME Eff HC = SME HC × SME Productive % | Hours Mode: SME Eff HC = SME HC × (SME Productive Hours / Productive Hours per HC)

Off-Roster Effective HC

Counts only the productive portion of off-roster HC.

Percent Mode: Off Eff HC = Off HC × Off Productive % | Hours Mode: Off Eff HC = Off HC × (Off Productive Hours / Productive Hours per HC)

Net HC Before Shrinkage

Supply after additions and deductions, before shrinkage is applied.

Net HC Before Shrinkage = Gross Available HC - Attrition HC - SME HC - Off-Roster HC + SME Eff HC + Off Eff HC

Effective HC

Available HC adjusted by actual shrinkage.

Effective HC = Net HC Before Shrinkage × (1 - Actual Shrinkage %)

OT Equivalent HC

Converts OT hours into HC equivalent.

OT Eq HC = OT Hours / OT Conversion Base Hours

Final Available HC

Total effective supply after shrinkage, OT, and nesting contribution.

Final Available HC = Effective HC + OT Eq HC + Nesting Effective HC

Monthly Weighted Roll-Up

Allocates weekly values into months based on actual number of calendar days falling in that month.

Monthly Metric = Σ(Weekly Metric × Days in That Month Portion) / Days Covered
